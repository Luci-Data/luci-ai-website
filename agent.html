Why So Many Professional Categories

A sale doesn't end at "yes."
LuciHome covers what comes after.

Once a buyer agrees to a deal, the work isn't over — financing has to be arranged, and the transaction has to be legally certified. Most agents handle this by juggling separate banks, separate notaries, and separate paperwork outside their control. LuciHome brings agents, agencies, notaries, and banks into the same ecosystem, so your deal doesn't lose momentum once the buyer says yes.

🤝
Agents & Agencies
Collaborate, share listings, and reach buyers across borders without working alone.
🏦
Banks
Your buyers can calculate financing directly on the listing, and get matched to a lender already familiar with the property.
🛡️
Notaries
Once a deal closes, it moves to a notary on the network with verified parties and valuation data already in place.

For you, this means less time chasing third parties and more time closing your next deal — the whole chain, from first contact to final signature, stays connected.

Beta Access

The beta is completely free.
No catch.

From the moment LuciHome's beta goes live until the official launch, every feature is open and free for every agent to explore — your profile, the collaboration network, AI price estimates, everything. There are no hidden tiers and no paywalls during this period.

After the official launch, free access becomes limited to a fixed number of spots per professional category. Agents who register now as Founding Members are guaranteed one of those spots, regardless of how much interest the platform receives before then.
