How Collaboration Works

Working together,
on your terms.

Collaboration only works if it's clear. Here are the questions agents ask us most.

Who sees my properties?
Only verified agents and agencies on the network — never the public directly, and never anyone outside LuciHome. You always own the listing.
Can I control who promotes them?
Yes. You approve every collaboration request before another agent can present your listing to their clients. Nothing happens without your consent.
How do we split the commission?
You and the collaborating agent agree on a split before the deal moves forward — clearly recorded on the platform, so there's no ambiguity at closing.
Can I refuse a collaboration?
Always. Every request can be declined with no penalty. You're never obligated to work with an agent you don't want to.
What if two agents bring the same client?
LuciHome timestamps buyer introductions, so the first verified contact is documented — removing the guesswork from disputed leads.

Why So Many Professional Categories

A sale doesn't end at "yes."
LuciHome covers what comes after.

Once a buyer agrees to a deal, the work isn't over — financing has to be arranged, and the transaction has to be legally certified. Most agents handle this by juggling separate banks, separate notaries, and separate paperwork outside their control. LuciHome brings agents, agencies, notaries, and banks into the same ecosystem, so your deal doesn't lose momentum once the buyer says yes.

🤝

Banks

Notaries

For you, this means less time chasing third parties and more time closing your next deal — the whole chain, from first contact to final signature, stays connected.

FAQ

Questions agents
actually ask.

Can I collaborate with multiple agents?
Yes, there's no limit. You can accept as many collaboration requests as make sense for your listings.
How are commissions handled?
You and the collaborating agent agree on a split before proceeding, recorded on the platform so both sides are protected at closing.
Can agencies invite individual agents?
Yes. Agencies on LuciHome can bring their agents onto the network under the agency profile or as independent profiles.
Are buyers verified?
Every buyer who contacts you through LuciHome has been identity-checked, cutting down on time-wasters.
Can I remove my listings anytime?
Yes, you're always in control of your own portfolio and can unpublish a listing at any time.
Can international agents access my properties?
Only if you allow it. Visibility across borders happens through collaboration requests you approve, not automatically.
Do I decide who collaborates with me?
Always. Every collaboration is opt-in on both sides.
Is there exclusivity?
No. LuciHome is designed around open collaboration rather than locking agents or listings into a single relationship.
