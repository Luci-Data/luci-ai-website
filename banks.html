The Problem

Buyers decide to finance
long before they reach a bank.

By the time a mortgage application lands on your desk, the buyer has already shopped around — often with the competition.

⏱️
You arrive too late in the journey
Buyers research properties and financing options for weeks before ever speaking to a bank. Without presence at that moment, you lose the lead to a faster competitor.
📉
No visibility into real buying intent
Generic ads reach people who may never buy. There is no signal telling you which buyers are actively evaluating a specific property right now.
🧩
Disconnected valuation and loan data
Property price, market valuation, and loan request live in separate systems, slowing down pre-approval and increasing risk on every file.
How It Works

From browsing
to pre-approved, on autopilot.

Three steps that turn property browsing into qualified financing leads for your bank.

01
Set up your mortgage products
Register your institution and load your current rates, loan terms, and eligibility criteria. LuciHome uses them to power live calculations across the platform.
02
Appear in every rate calculation
Every buyer who uses the built-in mortgage calculator on a listing sees your bank's offer, instantly, next to the property they're already interested in.
03
Receive the financing lead, pre-qualified
When a buyer requests a quote, you receive their details, the property, the AI-backed valuation, and their estimated monthly payment — ready for pre-approval.
