The Founding Member Program isn't a signup form — it's how we're measuring real interest in LuciHome before launch, and rewarding the people who believe in it early.

When Beta Launches

The beta version of LuciHome will be completely free for everyone to explore — every feature, every professional category, open to anyone who wants to see what the platform can do.

Why we're tracking interest now

Before launch, we want to understand exactly how many people — agents, agencies, notaries, banks, and buyers — are genuinely interested in a platform like LuciHome. Every person who registers interest helps us gauge that demand and prepare the platform accordingly.

This isn't about collecting signups for the sake of numbers. It's about knowing who to prioritize, who to keep informed, and who gets recognized as part of LuciHome's earliest community once we go live.
