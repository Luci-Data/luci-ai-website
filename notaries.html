The Problem

Transactions arrive on your desk
without context.

By the time a file reaches a notary, critical information has already been lost, duplicated, or never verified at all.

📄
Incomplete or unverified documentation
Files arrive from different agents and agencies, in different formats, often missing details that should have been confirmed earlier in the process.
🔍
No visibility into the deal history
You rarely see how a price was negotiated, who was involved, or whether the valuation was realistic — you only see the final paperwork.
🌍
Cross-border deals are harder to trust
When a buyer or seller is foreign, verifying identity, funds, and intent takes longer — and the tools to do it efficiently don't exist yet.
How It Works

One network,
verified from the start.

Three steps that bring clarity and trust to every file before it reaches your desk.

01
Create your notary profile
