Mortgage Rate Calculator

Last updated: June 2026

This page applies specifically to the Mortgage Rate Calculator available on LuciHome property listing pages and on the LuciHome for Banks section.

Estimates Only

The Mortgage Rate Calculator provides an approximate estimate of a possible monthly payment, based solely on the property price, down payment, interest rate, and loan term entered by the user. The result is a mathematical calculation and does not constitute a loan offer, a pre-approval, or any form of financial commitment from LuciHome or any partner bank.

Not a Loan Offer

No information generated by the calculator should be interpreted as an offer to lend, a guarantee of loan approval, or a final interest rate. Actual mortgage terms, interest rates, fees, and eligibility are determined exclusively by the relevant lending institution following its own assessment of the applicant and the property.

Interest Rates Shown

Interest rates displayed or used as defaults within the calculator are illustrative and may not reflect the current rate offered by any specific bank. Users should confirm actual rates and terms directly with the relevant financial institution before making any decision.

No Financial Advice

The calculator is provided for general informational purposes only and does not constitute financial, legal, or tax advice. LuciHome recommends that users consult a licensed financial advisor or mortgage specialist before making any property financing decision.
