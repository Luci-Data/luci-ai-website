Why LuciHome Exists
Buying property abroad should feel as simple as buying it at home.

Today, buyers, agencies, banks, and legal professionals all work in separate systems — different portals, different languages, different rules, no shared thread connecting them. LuciHome brings them together into one platform, so an international purchase can feel as natural as a local one.

One Platform. Every Professional. One Transaction.
The Problem

International buyers exist.
They just can't find your listings.

Every year, millions of people buy property abroad — but the tools to connect them with the right agency don't exist yet.

🌐
Your listings are invisible internationally
Local portals reach local buyers. There is no single platform that connects agencies worldwide with international buyers actively looking to purchase.
💸
Buyers don't know what fair price looks like
When buying abroad, buyers have no reference point for local prices. This creates hesitation, long negotiation cycles, and lost deals for agencies.
🤝
Trust between borders is broken
Without verification systems, international buyers distrust foreign agencies. Without trust, there are no cross-border transactions.

FAQ

Questions agencies
ask us first.

Is LuciHome free?

Yes. The beta is 100% free for every professional category, with no hidden tiers. After the official launch, free access becomes limited to a fixed number of spots per category — Founding Members are guaranteed one regardless.

Which countries are supported?

LuciHome is built for 50+ countries at launch, with an architecture designed to keep expanding into new markets over time.

How are agencies verified?

Every agency's license and credentials are reviewed before their profile goes live, so international buyers can trust who they're contacting from day one.

Can individual agents join?

Yes. Independent agents can join the network, collaborate with agencies, and bring buyers to listings — or refer their own properties into other agencies' networks.

Is AI valuation available everywhere?

AI valuation is rolling out market by market alongside our country coverage, starting with our priority launch regions.

Can buyers contact agencies directly?

Yes. Buyers reach out to agencies directly through the platform, arriving with a verified profile, budget, and timeline already attached.

How does collaboration between professionals work?

Agencies, notaries, banks, and other professionals operate on a shared transaction file — so a deal can move from listing to signature without leaving the platform.

Beta Access

The beta is completely free.
No catch.

From the moment LuciHome's beta goes live until the official launch, every feature is open and free for every agency to explore — full listing tools, the agent network, AI price estimates, everything. There are no hidden tiers and no paywalls during this period.

After the official launch, free access becomes limited to a fixed number of spots per professional category. Agencies that register now as Founding Members are guaranteed one of those spots, regardless of how much interest the platform receives before then.
