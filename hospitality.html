Why Hospitality Is Part Of LuciHome

Real estate extends
far beyond buying and selling.

People travel to visit developments. Investors fly to meet developers. Buyers spend days exploring new cities before making one of the biggest decisions of their lives. Architects, designers, engineers and business partners regularly travel for projects. Hospitality naturally becomes part of this journey.

Our approach

Instead of creating another booking platform, LuciHome integrates hospitality into the same ecosystem that already connects real estate professionals around the world — so a developer, an agent, a buyer, and a hotel manager can all be part of the same conversation.

FAQ

Questions hospitality
businesses ask us most.

Quick answers before you register.

Is LuciHome trying to replace Booking platforms?

No. Hospitality is included as part of the LuciHome ecosystem, not as a replacement for existing booking platforms.

Who can join?

Any legitimate hospitality business, including hotels, guesthouses, apartments, villas, resorts and similar accommodation providers.

Is Beta free?

Yes. Beta access is completely free.

What is a Founding Member?

The first 500 businesses joining the program will receive a permanent Founding Member badge together with six months of free access after the official launch.

Will Hospitality have its own workspace?

Yes. Every business receives a dedicated Hospitality Workspace with management tools, analytics and future AI features.

Can I manage multiple properties?

Yes. The workspace is designed to support multiple accommodation properties.
