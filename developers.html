Working Together

How Developers And Agents
Work Together

You stay in control of your project at every step. Here's exactly how the relationship with agents works — no guesswork.

Who actually sells my project?
Any agent on the LuciHome network can request to represent your project. You see who they are, their track record, and their market before anything goes live.
Do I approve every agent?
Yes. Every agent who wants to represent your project sends a request. Nothing is published under your name without your approval first.
How many agents can represent one project?
As many or as few as you want — one agent for an exclusive local push, or dozens across different countries for maximum international reach. You decide the setup.
Can I say no?
Always. Declining an agent's request costs you nothing and doesn't affect your visibility to other agents or buyers.
Is exclusivity possible?
Yes — you can choose to work with a single agent per market, or open the project to the full network. Both models are supported.
What happens after approval?
Approved agents promote your project to their own buyer network, both locally and abroad, while you keep full visibility over every lead they bring in.

Frequently Asked

Questions, answered.

Can multiple agents represent one project?
Yes. You can open your project to as many agents as you want, or keep it exclusive to one — the choice is entirely yours.
Do I approve agents?
Yes. Every agent who wants to represent your project needs your approval before they can promote it.
Do buyers contact me directly?
Yes. Buyer inquiries reach you with their profile, budget, and timeline already attached, whether they come through an agent or directly.
Are commissions handled outside LuciHome?
Commission arrangements are agreed directly between you and the agents you work with — LuciHome doesn't take a cut during the beta.
Can I edit projects anytime?
Yes. You can update pricing, availability, media, and details on your projects whenever you need to.
Can I upload future developments?
Yes. You can list projects that are still in planning or under construction, so agents and buyers can discover them early.
Can international buyers reserve units?
Yes. Buyers can express interest and move toward a reservation directly through the platform, with financing and notarization connected in the same flow.
