For Developers

Built to move more units,
in more markets.

LuciHome isn't another listing portal. It's an active sales network — with agents, buyers, financing, and legal tools connected in one ecosystem built around your project.

🌐
A global agent network that sells for you
Agents across the platform can pick up your project and sell it to their own buyers — nationally and internationally. You're no longer limited to the agents you already know.
🏗️
Your own dedicated developer section
Developers have a separate, dedicated space on LuciHome — not buried in regular listings. Your profile, your projects, your track record — presented professionally to agents and buyers alike.
🤖
AI price validation that builds buyer confidence
International buyers are often hesitant about unfamiliar markets. LuciHome's AI price estimator validates your pricing against real market data — reducing objections and shortening decision cycles.
🏦
Financing built into every listing
Buyers can calculate mortgage financing directly on your project page. Remove one of the biggest barriers to purchase — uncertainty about affordability — before they even contact you.
⚡
From listing to signed contract, in one place
Once a buyer is ready, the deal moves seamlessly to financing and notarization — all within the LuciHome network. No lost momentum, no chasing third parties.
