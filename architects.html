The Problem

Great design is invisible
when the right clients can't find you.

Architects and designers do some of the most important work in real estate — yet they remain the most disconnected from the process of buying, selling, and developing property.

📂
Your portfolio exists — but not where clients are looking
Instagram, Behance, personal websites — your work is scattered across platforms that aren't connected to where real estate decisions are made. Developers and agencies aren't searching design platforms when they need professionals.
🔗
No direct line to developers and agencies
Architects and interior designers typically find projects through referrals and word of mouth — a network that takes years to build and limits every professional to the contacts they already have.
📉
Design is added last — not built in from the start
In most real estate transactions, architectural and design expertise comes in too late. By the time a developer or seller thinks about it, opportunities to add real value have already been missed.

For Architects & Designers

Built to put your expertise
at the centre of real estate

LuciHome is built around the idea that design and architecture are not add-ons to a real estate transaction — they are central to it. Here's how the platform supports that.

🏗️
Direct access to developers with active projects
Developers on LuciHome are listing real projects and looking for the right collaborators. Your profile gives you visibility at the exact moment they are searching — not through a referral chain that may never reach you.
🏢
Agencies recommend you directly to clients
Agents and agencies on the platform can recommend your services to sellers preparing a property for listing, to buyers planning renovations, and to investors looking to increase asset value. A direct referral channel with no intermediary.
🌍
International visibility, not just local
LuciHome operates across 50+ countries. Developers and property owners from other markets who are investing internationally can discover your work, expanding your reach far beyond your local referral network.

Property staging and pre-sale design

AI valuation shows what good design is worth
