Why LuciHome Exists

Because talent alone
has never been enough.

You can design the best-planned building in your city, and still lose the project to whichever architect happened to be in the room when the developer was hiring. LuciHome exists because being found shouldn't depend on luck, timing, or who you already know.

A real example

Imagine this: a developer in Lisbon is finalizing a 40-unit residential project and needs an architect who understands sustainable materials and has worked on similar-scale developments before. Today, they ask around, scroll LinkedIn, and call three agencies hoping someone knows a name. On LuciHome, they filter by specialization, project type, and country — and your profile is simply there, ready to be found, with the work to back it up.

The Problem

Great design is invisible
when the right clients can't find you.

Architects and designers do some of the most important work in real estate — yet they remain the most disconnected from the process of buying, selling, and developing property.

📂
Your portfolio exists — but not where clients are looking
Instagram, Behance, personal websites — your work is scattered across platforms that aren't connected to where real estate decisions are made. Developers and agencies aren't searching design platforms when they need professionals.
🔗
No direct line to developers and agencies
Architects and interior designers typically find projects through referrals and word of mouth — a network that takes years to build and limits every professional to the contacts they already have.
📉
Design is added last — not built in from the start
In most real estate transactions, architectural and design expertise comes in too late. By the time a developer or seller thinks about it, opportunities to add real value have already been missed.

FAQ

Questions architects
ask us most.

Quick answers before you register.

Is LuciHome really free during the beta?

Yes. Every feature — your profile, portfolio, and visibility to developers, agencies, and buyers — is completely free until the official launch on August 30, 2026. There are no hidden tiers during this period.

What happens to my access after the official launch?

Free spots become limited per professional category after launch. Registering now as a Founding Member guarantees you one of those spots, ahead of anyone who joins afterward.

Do I need a large portfolio to join?

No. Your profile grows with you — you can start with a handful of projects and add certifications, awards, and new work over time. What matters is that your specialization and countries served are accurate, so the right people find you.

How is this different from Behance or a personal website?

Behance and personal websites showcase your work, but they aren't where developers, agencies, and property owners are searching when they need to hire. LuciHome puts your profile directly inside the real estate transaction itself.

Can I work internationally through LuciHome?

Yes. LuciHome operates across 50+ countries, so developers and buyers investing outside their home market can discover and reach you directly — well beyond your local referral network.
