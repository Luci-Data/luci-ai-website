Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, which cookies LuciHome ("we", "us", "our") uses on lucihome.com (the "Site"), and how you can control them. It should be read together with our Privacy Policy.

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites function properly, remember your preferences, and understand how visitors use a site.

How We Use Cookies

When you first visit the Site, you'll see a cookie banner asking you to accept or reject non-essential cookies. Your choice is saved in your browser's local storage so we don't ask again on future visits, until you clear your browser data.

We use two categories of cookies:

Category Purpose Can be rejected?
Essential Required for the Site to function correctly, including remembering your cookie consent choice. No — these are necessary for basic functionality.
Analytics Help us understand how visitors use the Site (pages viewed, time on page, approximate location, device type) via Google Analytics 4, so we can improve the platform. Yes — only activated if you accept via the cookie banner.

We do not currently use cookies for advertising, retargeting, or for selling data to third parties.

Third-Party Cookies

Analytics cookies on the Site are set by Google Analytics 4. Google may process this data in accordance with its own privacy and cookie policies. We do not control how Google uses this data outside the scope of the analytics service it provides to us.

How to Control Cookies

  • Use the Accept or Reject buttons on the cookie banner shown when you first visit the Site.
  • Clear your browser's local storage or cookies at any time to reset your choice and see the banner again.
  • Adjust your browser settings to block cookies entirely; note this may affect how some parts of the Site function.

Changes to This Policy

As LuciHome adds new features ahead of its beta launch, this Cookie Policy may be updated to reflect any new cookies or tracking technologies introduced. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our use of cookies, contact us at contact@lucihome.com or through our Contact page.
